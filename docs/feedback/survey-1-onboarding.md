# Survey 1 — Onboarding (after first prediction)

**Goal:** capture first-impression friction before the user normalises to the app.

**When to send:** 24-72 hours after the user submits their first match prediction.

**Build instructions:** create a new Google Form titled "WC2026 Predictions — first impressions". Copy the description block below into the form's settings, then add each question in order. Field types are noted in **bold** before each one.

---

## Form description

> You've made your first prediction on WC2026 Predictions — thank you. ~2 minutes of feedback now will make this much better for the next person. All questions are optional and the survey is anonymous unless you choose to share your name at the end.

---

## Questions

### Q1. Which device did you use to make your first prediction?
**Type:** Multiple choice (single-select). **Required:** No.

Options:
- iPhone
- Android phone
- Tablet (iPad or other)
- Laptop or desktop
- Mix of devices

---

### Q2. Within your first 30 seconds in the app, how clear was it what you were supposed to do?
**Type:** Linear scale 1–5. **Required:** No.

- 1 = "Lost — I had no idea"
- 5 = "Crystal clear"

Add immediately below this as a separate field:

### Q2b. (Optional) What helped or hurt that first impression?
**Type:** Paragraph (long text). **Required:** No.

Placeholder: *"Only fill in if you picked 1, 2, or 3 above. Even one line helps."*

---

### Q3. Was anything in your first 5 minutes confusing, or did anything almost make you give up?
**Type:** Paragraph (long text). **Required:** No.

Placeholder: *"It's fine if nothing did. If something did, even one line helps."*

---

### Q4. Did you understand how points are awarded?
**Type:** Multiple choice (single-select). **Required:** No.

Options:
- Yes, fully
- Sort of, but I'd want to re-read it
- No, I'm not sure how scoring works

---

### Q5. Anything else from your first session you want to flag?
**Type:** Paragraph (long text). **Required:** No.

Placeholder: *"Bug, confusion, surprise — whatever's on your mind."*

---

### Q6. Want to share your name and email? (Optional — only if you're OK with us replying to clarify.)

**Type:** Two short answer fields, both **optional**.

- Field A: "Name (optional)" — Short answer
- Field B: "Email (optional)" — Short answer (set the validation to "Email" so Forms catches typos)

---

## Form settings to flip on

- **Confirmation message:** "Thanks — this is the kind of feedback that actually moves the app forward."
- **Show progress bar:** off (only 6 fields, doesn't help)
- **Limit to 1 response:** off (we're not requiring sign-in)
- **Collect email addresses:** **off** — Q6 already asks optionally; turning the auto-collect on would defeat the anonymity promise in the description block.
- **Restrict to organisation:** off (public)

---

## Total length expected

6 fields. About **2 minutes** to complete for an attentive user. The hard cap on speed is Q3 + Q5 + Q2b, which are all optional free text — most users will skip at least one of those.
